Fabric Defect Detection



A Fabric Defect Detection Project focuses on identifying and categorizing defects in fabric during the manufacturing process, ensuring high-quality standards for the final product. This can be achieved through various methods like image processing, machine learning, or deep learning techniques. The goal is to automate the detection of defects in fabrics (like textiles used for clothing, upholstery, etc.) and provide a fast, accurate way to identify problems, improving product quality and reducing waste.
